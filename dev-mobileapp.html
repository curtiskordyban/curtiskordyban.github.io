Mobile Application Development

  • Photo by Goran Ivos on Unsplash

Throughout my time spent at NAIT, I have always been interested in learning about mobile application development. Now that I have the free time to peruse the things that are of interest to me, mobile apps are at the top of my bucket list.

One of the great attributes about software development is the access to free content and courses. The ability to work at your own pace and from home is something that I value. One of my goals for the year 2021 is to release a responsive application in both the iOS and Android environment by the end of the year.
