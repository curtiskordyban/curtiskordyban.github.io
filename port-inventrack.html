What is Inventrack?

Inventrack is an inventory management system. This application will keep track of inventory quantity, the location of products on hand and generate orders to be picked for customers.

Why create Inventrack?

To me, it was a no brainer. The fact that I have spent the previous 10 years working at a warehouse. I wanted to turn my irrelevant work experience into something relevant. I believe that I have a thorough understanding of what is necessary to develop a robust inventory tracking system.

I knew that I wanted to create a system that could combine my knowledge of databases, ASP.NET core forms and web-based applications. An Inventory management system could not be more suitable.

Who am I Targeting?

In my experience, I have discovered multiple small companies that still manage their inventory by use of spreadsheets. After conversing with a friend at a different company, I learned that he fulfills orders the old-fashioned way, by paper.

Not all companies can afford the investment of the system and RFID scanners. I hope to bridge that gap by offering an affordable solution by enabling employees to complete orders by use of their smartphones and eliminate the cost of RFID scanners all together.
